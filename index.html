Frequently asked questions about Notion Slack lists sync

How do I sync my Notion database with Slack lists?

To sync Notion with Slack lists: First, sign up for ListSync and connect your Notion workspace using OAuth authentication. Second, connect your Slack workspace. Third, select which Notion database you want to sync with which Slack list. Fourth, activate the sync. The entire guided setup process takes just a few minutes with no coding required.

Does ListSync sync both ways between Notion and Slack?

Yes, ListSync provides true bidirectional synchronization. When you update a task in your Notion database, it automatically syncs to Slack lists. When you or your team changes a task in Slack Lists, it syncs back to Notion immediately. This eliminates manual copying in both directions and keeps both platforms perfectly synchronized.

How fast do updates sync between Notion and Slack lists?

Updates sync in real-time. When you change a task status, assignee, due date, or any field in either Notion or Slack lists, the other platform updates automatically within seconds. You don't need to manually refresh or wait — changes appear immediately.

What Notion properties can sync to Slack lists?

ListSync supports all core Notion database property types including Text, Number, Select (dropdown), Multi-select, Date, Person (for assignees), Checkbox, URL, Email, Phone, and Status fields. These properties map directly to the corresponding Slack list field types like completion status, text, user, date, and select fields.

How much does Notion to Slack lists sync cost?

ListSync costs €50 per month with unlimited Notion databases, unlimited Slack lists, and unlimited sync actions included. There are no per-task fees, no per-user charges, and no hidden costs. You get everything for one flat monthly price and can cancel anytime without penalties.

Can I sync multiple Notion databases to different Slack lists?

Yes, the €50/month Early Access plan includes unlimited database-to-list sync pairs. You can connect your project management database to your team's main Slack list, your task tracker to a separate list, your client database to another list — as many connections as your team needs with no additional charges.

What happens if I edit the same task in both Notion and Slack simultaneously?

If the same task is edited in both platforms at nearly the same time, the most recent change wins based on timestamps. For example, if you update a task in Notion at 2:00:00 PM and someone updates it in Slack at 2:00:05 PM, the Slack change overwrites the Notion change. All changes are logged in our system for audit purposes.

Is my data secure when syncing Notion and Slack?

Yes, ListSync uses end-to-end encryption for all data transfers between Notion and Slack. We never store your Notion database content or Slack list items permanently on our servers — data only passes through during sync operations. All authentication uses secure OAuth protocols, and we never see or store your Notion or Slack passwords.

Do I need a paid Slack plan to use Slack lists?

Yes, Slack lists is a premium feature only available on paid Slack plans including Pro, Business+, and Enterprise Grid tiers. Free Slack workspaces do not have access to the Slack Lists feature. You'll need to upgrade your Slack plan to at least Pro ($7.25/user/month) to use Lists and sync with Notion.
